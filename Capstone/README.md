# Capstone

The capstone bundles the seven steps into three artifacts:

1. **`NovaBev_BTP_Portfolio.pdf`** — 8-slide deck:
   - Slide 1: Cover + the NovaBev one-paragraph story
   - Slides 2–8: One slide per step (Step 0 through Step 7), each containing the step's hero screenshot, the resume language, and a one-sentence statement of what it shows

2. **5-minute demo video** — screen capture walking through:
   - The deployed NovaBev order Fiori app (Step 1)
   - The CPI iFlow Monitor showing the Completed message (Step 2)
   - The BPA approval task in My Inbox (Step 5)
   - The live SAC dashboard filtering by company code (Step 7)
   - End with a 30-second whiteboard of the architecture (`architecture.png`)

3. **Live links** — embedded in the resume header and LinkedIn featured section:
   - Live deployed Fiori order app URL
   - Live SAC dashboard URL

## The one-paragraph story

> At NovaBev — a beverage company running three company codes — I built the order-capture app in CAP and Fiori, integrated confirmed orders to the logistics partner over CPI, governed the landscape with subaccounts and role collections, secured it through IAS trust and XSUAA, automated order approvals in SAP Build, modeled a clean-core promotion object in RAP, and delivered sales analytics in Datasphere and SAC.
